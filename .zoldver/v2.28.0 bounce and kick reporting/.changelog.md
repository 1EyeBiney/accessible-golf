## [2.28.0] - 2026-03-16
### Added
- **Lateral Ground Mechanics (The "Kick"):** The engine now simulates how side spin grips the turf upon landing. Shots with high side spin will bounce and roll laterally (kicking left for hooks/draws, right for slices/fades).
- **Caddy Kick Quantification:** The Caddy's shot narrative has been updated to explicitly report the lateral ground movement, ensuring blind players can accurately map the turf interaction in their mental model (e.g., "rolls 5 and kicks 3 yds right").## [2.27.0] - 2026-03-16
### Changed
- **Gyroscopic Wind Physics:** Overhauled how wind affects ball flight in `calculateShot`.
- **Wind Base Nerf:** Increased the divisor for base wind effect, preventing the "parachute" ballooning effect on high-trajectory shots. A 3mph crosswind will now push a perfectly struck ball ~4 yards instead of 17.
- **Backspin Stability:** Added `gyroMod`. High backspin (>4000 RPM) now "cuts" through the wind, reducing lateral and vertical wind drift by up to 30%. Low backspin makes the ball more susceptible to wind.
- **Spin Amplification:** Side spin and crosswind now interact. Hitting a slice into a right-to-left wind will cause the spin to "fight" the wind, keeping the ball straighter. Hitting a slice with a left-to-right wind will amplify the curve.## [2.26.4] - 2026-03-16
