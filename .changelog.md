## [2.12.0] - 2026-03-15
### Added
- **Stance Alignment Mechanics:** Players can now open or close their stance to control swing path and deliberately shape shots (Fade/Draw bias).
- **Alignment Physics:** Open stances add positive side-spin (slice bias); closed stances add negative side-spin (hook bias).
### Changed
- **Keybindings:** Replaced the 5-degree aiming modifier. `Shift + Left/Right Arrow` now controls Stance Alignment (Severe Closed, Closed, Neutral, Open, Severe Open). Standard `Left/Right Arrow` presses handle all rotational aiming in 1-degree increments.
- **Caddy Reports:** Pre-shot stance reports and post-shot telemetry now include both ball position (Front/Back) and alignment (Open/Closed).## [2.11.0] - 2026-03-15
### Added
- **Modular Narrative Generator:** Replaced the static post-shot readout with a dynamic, broadcast-style description combining Contact Quality, Trajectory, and Shot Shape (e.g., "A flushed, piercing draw").
- **Quantified Wind Effects:** The engine now isolates and reports the exact yardage the wind affected the ball's flight (e.g., "Wind pushed it 12 yards short and 8 yards right").
- **Split Reporting System:** The immediate post-shot audio now focuses purely on the natural narrative and wind effects, while the `KeyC` Caddy Report retains all the raw telemetry (RPMs, milliseconds, percentages) for deep swing analysis.## [2.10.0] - 2026-03-15
### Added
- **Stance Mechanics:** Players can now move the ball forward or backward in their stance across 5 positions (Far Forward, Forward, Neutral, Back, Far Back).
- **Trajectory & Spin Modifiers:** Stance position directly alters `dynamicLoft` (+/- 10 degrees) and `backspinRPM` (+/- 1000 RPM).
- **Keybindings:** Mapped `Home` (move ball forward) and `End` (move ball backward) to control the stance while in the Idle state.
- **Auto-Reset:** Stance automatically resets to Neutral after every completed shot to prevent accidental mishits on the next swing.## [2.9.0] - 2026-03-15
### Added
- **Rotational Aiming:** Players can now rotate their stance using the `Left` and `Right` arrow keys.
- **Precision Modifiers:** Pressing `Shift` + `Left/Right` shifts the aim by 5 degrees at a time, while standard presses shift by 1 degree.
- **Trigonometric Dispersion:** The `lateralX` landing position now incorporates the `aimAngle`. The engine calculates the lateral offset using `Math.tan` based on the total distance of the shot.
- **Aim Reset:** The player's aim automatically resets to 0 (dead straight) after every completed shot.## [2.8.0] - 2026-03-15
### Added
- **Dynamic Wind Physics:** Introduced randomized wind vectors (`windX` for crosswinds, `windY` for headwinds/tailwinds) ranging from -15 to +15 mph.
- **Trajectory Interaction:** Wind effect is multiplied by `hangTimeSecs`. High-loft shots (Wedges, "Fat" strikes) are heavily affected by the wind; low-loft shots (Drivers, "Thin" stingers) pierce through it.
- **Weather Report (Key W):** Mapped the `W` key to announce the current wind speed and direction while in the Idle state.
- **Pre-Shot Randomization:** Wind conditions regenerate automatically after every completed shot.## [2.7.0] - 2026-03-15
### Added
- **Spin Rate Physics (RPM):** Introduced explicit Backspin and Sidespin calculations derived from Hinge and Impact timing.
- **Spin-to-Roll Linkage:** Roll distance is now a direct function of landing velocity and Backspin RPM rather than a flat multiplier.
- **Caddy Report Expansion:** Added "Spin Metrics" to the Caddy Report (Key C) to provide feedback on ball rotation.## [2.6.0] - 2026-03-15
### Changed
- **Polyphonic Audio Overlap:** The flight "whoosh" now persists and fades during the first 300ms of the landing phase, preventing sharp auditory cut-offs.
- **Wider Bounce Spacing:** Increased the base gap and distance-scaling factor to ensure skips remain distinct even on high-velocity shots.
- **Improved Decay Physics:** Updated the timing decay to 0.85 and volume to 0.8 to keep late-sequence bounces audible and rhythmic.
- **Independent Gain Nodes:** Each bounce now utilizes a distinct gain envelope to maximize volume without clipping the master output.## [2.5.0] - 2026-03-15
### Changed
- **Bounce Interval Physics**: Recalibrated the timing between bounces to prevent "clustering." 
- **Dynamic Gaps**: The initial gap between the first and second bounce now scales with `rollDistance`, and the decay factor has been eased to ensure skips are distinct even at high roll speeds.
- **Audio Sequencing**: Refined the transition between the final bounce and the start of the "Roll" rumble to ensure zero auditory overlap.## [2.4.0] - 2026-03-15
### Changed
- **Bounce Density Physics**: Rebalanced the bounce-count formula to better reflect low-trajectory clubs.
- **Trajectory Integration**: Added `dynamicLoft` as a variable in determining bounce frequency. A well-struck Driver (10-12 degrees) now consistently produces 4-5 skips on firm fairways.## [2.3.0] - 2026-03-15

### Added
- **Dynamic Bounce Audio:** Replaced the static landing thud with a dynamic bounce sequence.
- **Trajectory-Based Ground Interaction:** The number of bounces, the timing between them, and their volume now scale mathematically with the `rollDistance`. High-loft clubs (Wedges) produce a single heavy thud, while low-loft clubs (Driver, Woods) produce multiple cascading, skipping bounces before the roll out.## [2.1.0] - 2026-03-15

### Added
- **Golf Bag Array:** Introduced 11 selectable clubs (Driver through Sand Wedge), each with specific base distances, lofts, and roll percentages.
- **Club Selection:** Mapped `PageUp` and `PageDown` to cycle through clubs while in State 0 (Idle), complete with `window.announce` feedback.
- **Flight Phase (State 5):** Added a new state that locks out keyboard input while the ball is "in the air."
- **Trajectory & Roll Physics:** Introduced dynamic loft and roll calculations. Fat/Thin hinge misses now directly impact hang time and roll-out distance.
- **Audio Sequencer:** Replaced instant impact reporting with a sequenced audio event: Strike (triangle wave) -> Flight Wind (white noise) -> Landing (sine thud) -> Roll (brown noise rumble) -> Caddie Reveal.
