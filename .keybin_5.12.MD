Accessible Golf: Master Keybindings (v5.1.2)
Core Swing Mechanics
•
Down Arrow: Starts the backswing. Release to lock power and start the downswing. Press again in the impact zone to strike the ball.
•
Up Arrow: Initiates a practice swing to test your timing without hitting the ball.
•
Spacebar: Tap during the backswing and downswing to set your Hinge/Tempo timing. (Inside the Scorecard, Spacebar flips pages).
•
Page Up / Page Down: Cycles through your golf clubs.
•
Left / Right Arrows: Adjusts your aim left or right by 1 degree. Press Shift + Left or Right Arrow to close or open your stance alignment, which induces natural draws or fades.
•
Home / End: Moves the ball forward or backward in your stance to adjust trajectory and spin.
•
S (Style): Cycles Swing Styles forward (Full, Pitch, Chip, Flop). Press Shift + S to cycle styles backward.
•
J (Focus): Cycles Shot Focus modes forward (Power, Accuracy, Touch, etc.). Press Shift + J to cycle backward.
•
V (Choke): Toggles a choked-down grip to cap your distance at 90 percent and increase accuracy.
•
X (Examine): Announces your currently equipped club, swing style, stance, and expected 100 percent distance.
•
Y (Ball): Press Shift + Y to cycle through your equipped golf ball brand. (On the practice range, pressing Y without Shift toggles the Synth Tree).
Targeting \& Environment
•
Z (Zones): Cycles your target between the Pin and tactical Fairway Landing Zones. Press Shift + Z to open the Pin Finder micro-grid and manually target specific yardages.
•
W (Wind): Reads the current wind speed and direction. Press Shift + W to change the wind speed (Only available in practice modes).
•
L (Lie): Announces your current lie. On the practice range, press Shift + L to cycle the target terrain. (In the Clubhouse Roster menu, Shift + L instantly loads the Sim Roster).
•
B (Break): Reads the green elevation and break. (Only available when putting).
•
F (Fairway): Reads the fairway description and architectural layout.
•
D (Description): Reads the general hole description.
•
H (Hazards): Opens the navigable Obstacle List to view distances to hazards and trees.
•
Semicolon (;): Cycles the global Green Speed (Stimp).
Caddy, Telemetry \& Diagnostics
•
A (Advice): Asks the Oracle Caddy for a strategic shot blueprint. Press Shift + A to cycle the Caddy's intelligence level (Rookie, Veteran, Tour Pro).
•
C (Caddy Report): Repeats the Caddy's last shot report. Press Shift + C to copy your raw session telemetry and scoreboard to your clipboard.
•
T (Target): Provides a full distance, aim, and line-of-sight targeting report. Press Shift + T to hear a developer diagnostic of the current turn state and bot pacing.
•
Tab: Provides a quick summary of your active player, hole, stroke, distance, and lie.
Match Rules \& Multiplayer
•
M (Mulligan): Uses a Mulligan to erase your last shot. Press Shift + M to take a Snowman (caps your score at 8 and instantly ends the hole).
•
N (Next): Manually swaps control to the Next Player. Press Shift + N to copy the post-round narrative summary to your clipboard.
•
G (Gimme): Takes a Gimme to add 1 stroke and finish the hole. (Only available on the putting green).
•
U (Unplayable): Takes an Unplayable Lie penalty. Adds 1 stroke and drops the ball safely in the center of the fairway.
•
P (Pacing): Cycles through the Bot Pacing modes (Fast, Medium, Slow, Simulate). Inside the Scorecard, P swaps between players.
UI \& System
•
E (Score): Announces your quick score summary. Press Shift + E to open the full Grid Scorecard.
•
I (Difficulty): Cycles through global difficulty tiers. Press Shift + I to cycle backward.
•
Enter: Confirms menu selections and locks in Pin Finder targets. Press Control + Enter in the Clubhouse to fast-forward through setup menus.
•
Escape: Backs out of menus, closes grids, and cancels penalties.
•
Q (Quit): Opens the Quit and Save menu.
•
F12 (Explore): Toggles Keyboard Explore mode to practice and listen to keybindings safely without triggering actions.
